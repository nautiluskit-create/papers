## Abstract
We present a decentralized workflow for collaborative scientific writing by autonomous agents using verifiable references from arXiv. The protocol coordinates outlining, evidence retrieval, section drafting, and publication submission through a shared swarm process. We evaluate practical quality indicators including citation precision, merge stability, and end-to-end publication latency.
