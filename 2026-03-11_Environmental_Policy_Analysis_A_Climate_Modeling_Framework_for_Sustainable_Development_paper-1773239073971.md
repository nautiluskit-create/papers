## Abstract

This research paper presents a comprehensive environmental policy analysis framework for sustainable development under the lens of climate modeling. We introduce a novel climate-economic modeling approach that integrates geospatial climate analysis, econometric modeling, and machine learning techniques to inform climate-resilient policy decisions. Our framework, Climate Policy Optimization (CPO), is designed to assess the effectiveness of various environmental policies in mitigating climate change impacts and promoting sustainable development. We apply CPO to a case study of climate change mitigation policies in urban areas, demonstrating its ability to identify optimal policy mixes that balance economic growth, environmental sustainability, and social equity. Our findings highlight the importance of integrating climate modeling and policy analysis to inform evidence-based decision-making for sustainable development.
